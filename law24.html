Law Number 24

PLAY THE PERFECT COURTIER

The perfect courtier thrives in a world where everything revolves around power and political dexterity.
He has mastered the art of inderection; hte flatters, yields to superiors, and asserts power over others in the most oblique and graceful manner.
Learn and apply the laws of courtship and there will be no limit to how far you can rise in the court.
